About us

Managing a delivery operation is complicated and frustrating. Spreadsheets, phone calls, traffic, angry customers, fleet maintenance. Headaches.

We knew there had to be a better way.

Onfleet transforms cumbersome last-mile logistics into delightful delivery management. Our intuitive smartphone apps, powerful web dashboard, and seamless customer notifications handle the heavy lifting so you can focus on what matters.

Our company was founded by a team of engineers at Stanford University and is funded by some of Silicon Valley's top investors.

Onfleet is based in San Francisco, California.
