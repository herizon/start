Some common pricing questions

What is a "task"?

Tasks are the core unit of work that needs to be completed by your drivers. For example, a task could be a delivery or pickup of a good or service.

What if I go over my quota?

Exceeding the number of tasks in your plan will result in either a prorated overage charge based on your plan's current cost per task, or an automatic plan upgrade - we will always choose the cheaper option and it will happen automatically. The only exception is with the "Free" tier (you get bumped up to the "Starter" tier if you complete more than 75 tasks in one month). You can always see exactly what you'll be paying at the end of your billing cycle through the Billing section of your dashboard settings.

Is my task quota monthly or all-time?

It's a monthly quota, so the counter resets every 30 days. Tasks don't roll over to the next month.

What if I don't do any tasks in a given month?

We will never bill you if you don't complete any tasks in a given month (even if you had already started paying and have entered your credit card details). We will also automatically downgrade you to the appropriate plan for your current month's usage.

How does my free trial work?

There is no risk, no obligation, and no credit card required. You can use Onfleet for up to 75 tasks every month for free, for as long as you'd like. If you complete more tasks in a 30 day period, you will be required to enter your credit card information to continue using Onfleet.

How do I pay for Onfleet?

You will automatically be notified to enter your credit card information in your dashboard settings once you surpass 75 tasks in a single month. You are billed 30 days after you enter your credit card details, and your billing cycle is maintained thereafter. You can view your billing period in the Billing tab of your dashboard settings.
