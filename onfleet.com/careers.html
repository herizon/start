Careers

Onfleet's mission is to make every delivery efficient and delightful. We're reimagining how goods and services are delivered around the world and are looking for like-minded people who believe in the power of simple, intuitive products to solve some of the world's most complex problems. We're always looking for brilliant, enthusiastic people to join us so feel free to get in touch even if you don't see a position that fits you below.

Backend Engineer

Apply

We're looking for a top-notch backend engineer, with the opportunity to grow into much more. Backend experience and/or experience with building RESTful APIs is a must. Frontend experience is a plus.

We're building a modular platform that solves the hard technical problems around fleets of mobile workforces (e.g. route optimization, simplified dispatch UX/UI, real-time location tracking, driver mobile apps for multiple platforms). We provide an end-to-end solution for assigning work, tracking drivers and driver performance, and providing a delightful end-user experience with automatic SMS updates including accurate ETAs and a map to track deliveries en route. Incumbent fleet management software is antiquated, pre-smartphone era, and expensive.

Our backend is built using the latest technologies, including Node.js, Redis, MongoDB, and WebSockets. Our product is mission-critical, so reliability and stability are critical.

As our third engineering hire, you would work very closely with our product-focused founding team to bring a perfect delivery management experience to SMBs and other delivery-centric tech companies, enabling them to scale their businesses and delight their customers.

Requirements

  • 2-5 years of professional experience
  • BS in computer science or a reasonable approximation
  • Significant experience with TDD and integration testing
  • Careful engineer who likes to build fast and build well
  • Passionate self-starter ready to own key parts of our backend

Benefits

  • Employee stock options
  • Comprehensive health insurance
  • Flexible vacation policy
  • Gym membership
  • Public transportation pass or Lyft For Work credit
  • Apple MacBook Pro (or equivalent), display, keyboard, mouse and accessories
  • Regular team lunches
  • All-you-can-drink: wine, scotch and a sweet pour-over setup with a broad selection of locally-sourced, independently-roasted coffees

Inside Sales Rep

Apply

Our product is meticulously engineered and intuitively designed, and our customers, ranging from food and flower delivery businesses to moving & storage and dry cleaning services, really love us! Still, we still need detail-oriented, energetic Inside Sales Reps to make sure we can reach as many customers as possible to help them make their local deliveries more efficient and delightful!

Duties

  • Work with our marketing team and sales development reps to close accounts
  • Learn about pain points faced by potential leads and determine whether our product could solve their problems
  • Articulate Onfleet's value proposition very clearly and get customers to start trying the product for free
  • Communicate product feedback and prioritize with product team

Requirements

  • Bachelor's degree in related field required
  • 2-3 years Account Management, Customer Support, or Sales experience
  • Bright, articulate, patient, strong interpersonal skills, enthusiastic, friendly and detail-oriented
  • Must have exceptional written and verbal communication skills
  • Solution-oriented problem solver
  • Strong interpersonal skills, competitive and enthusiastic, while maintaining high integrity.
  • This role is for Hunters, not Farmers.

Benefits

  • Employee stock options
  • Comprehensive health insurance
  • Flexible vacation policy
  • Gym membership
  • Public transportation pass or Lyft For Work credit
  • Apple MacBook Air (or equivalent), display, keyboard, mouse and accessories
  • Regular team lunches
  • All-you-can-drink: wine, scotch and a sweet pour-over setup with a broad selection of locally-sourced, independently-roasted coffees
